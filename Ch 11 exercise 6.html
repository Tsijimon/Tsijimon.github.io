Hello. I will be doing my review on my favorite movie. It will be a quick, short one.
My review will be on Lord of the Rings.

The movie itself displays excellent effects, brilliant storytelling and dialogue and was overall faithful to the book. I highly recommend it to anyone who is interested in it.
